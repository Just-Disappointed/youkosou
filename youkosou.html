WELCOME TO POSTMORTEM ECONOMICS

Hello, Brethren.

Welcome to my blog: Postmortem Economics.

You may call me Tabulady because data in tabular format is all I see when I close my eyes.

This blog is where I want to keep all the ideas I have brewing and all the code I don't want to forget and; perhaps, even meet people who share my academic interests.

I will write a lot about Malawian macroeconomics for two reasons. First of all, there is a lot research potential in the Malawian economy. And we must not forget the eternal and most obvious question that no one can actually fully answer.



But, outside of this, there are many interesting questions to ask about the economy such as:

  • how does policymaking manifest in an environment of state fragility?
  • how effective is monetary policy in a low income country?
  • what does financial development look like in a country with shallow capital markets?
  • what does economic development look like in countries with large primary sectors?

Secondly, I am from Malawi and I am an economist.

Please note that there will also be articles about other countries and topics, all within the realm of economics, because I am curious about a great many things and I resist specialization.

Now, keep in mind that:

  • All the articles will be written with my playful irreverent voice.
  • Some of them will have companion pieces written in a proper academic format.
  • All of them will have citations because we are professionals here.
